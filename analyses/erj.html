The Thesis

Brazilian aerospace company transformed from near-bankruptcy to cash-generating machine, trading at 2.7x FCF while tech companies trade at 15-30x.

The Opportunity

Embraer operates in a structural monopoly for regional jets (70-150 seats), benefiting from:

  • Aging global fleet: 17+ year average age requiring replacement
  • Boeing distracted: 737 MAX issues, focused on larger aircraft
  • Airbus elsewhere: Concentrated on wide-body market
  • No new entrants: Capital intensity and certification barriers insurmountable

The Mispricing

The market is valuing ERJ as "old economy" aerospace when it's actually a monopolistic cash compounder:

  • Trading at 2.7x FCF vs. tech at 15-30x
  • $4.4B annual FCF with 70% conversion rate
  • $15B backlog providing 3+ years visibility
  • Market anchored to "Brazilian risk" rather than cash generation reality
